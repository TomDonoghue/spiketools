    spiketools.utils.base.combine_dicts¶

    spiketools.utils.base.combine_dicts(dicts)[source]¶

    Combine a list of dictionaries together.

    Parameters:
    dictslist of dict

    Dictionaries to combine.

    Returns:
    dict

    Combined dictionary.

    Examples

    Combine two dictionaries:

    >>> dict1 = {'a' : 1, 'b' : 2}
>>> dict2 = {'c' : 3, 'd' : 4}
>>> combine_dicts([dict1, dict2])
{'a': 1, 'b': 2, 'c': 3, 'd': 4}
